About Me
--------

**Minato Namikaze** (**波風ミナト**, Namikaze Minato) was the **Fourth
Hokage** (**四代目火影**, Yondaime Hokage, literally meaning: Fourth
Fire Shadow) of Konohagakure. He was renowned all over the world as
**Konoha’s Yellow Flash** (**木ノ葉の黄色い閃光**, Konoha no Kiiroi
Senkō, English TV: Yellow Flash of the Leaf). He died during the
Nine-Tailed Demon Fox’s Attack, sacrificing his life to seal a part of
the Nine-Tails into his newborn son, Naruto Uzumaki.
